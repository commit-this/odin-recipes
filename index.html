Recipes For the Discerning Uruk-Hai

As an impressively muscled warrior in the service of the fell wizard Saruman, you're in constant need of refreshment and nutrients to fuel your body, which in turn fuels the terrible war machine of the Dark Lord. But not just any old muck will do, for you are no rabble of mindless orcs. Your armor is thick and your shields are broad! Fortunately, there exist no less than one, maybe even two tolerable to okay recipes for the refined palate of the Uruk warrior.
