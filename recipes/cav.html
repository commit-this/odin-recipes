How do I stop a Rohirrim cavalry charge?

Description

You are part of a mighty Uruk host, 10,000 strong, armed with long pikes and on the verge of an overwhelming victory. On the first light of the fifth day, a bunch of guys on horses start running at you. You look at them, then at your weapon which happens to be highly effective against guys on horses, then you look back at them. How on Middle Earth will you get out of this pickle?

Ingredients

  • An army of ten thousand specially bred soldiers, whose sole purpose is war against the horse lords of Rohan
  • Long pointy sticks

Preparation

  1. Aim the pointy sticks at the guys on horses
  2. Stand still
Instructions too complicated, our great host was scattered and destroyed
