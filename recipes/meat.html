MEAT

Description

Okay, Uruk-Hai know what menus are. There's a really nice Uruk restaurant in downtown Bree, actually.

Ingredients

  • A band of orcs
  • Who need a breather
  • And have had nothing to eat but maggoty bread for three stinking days

Preparation

  1. Run for days
  2. Stop for a breather
  3. Get a fire going
  4. Behead the first orc who looks at the hobbits as if they're cartoon turkey legs
  5. Carefully remove the menu you are carrying in your special menu pocket and write "meat" on it
